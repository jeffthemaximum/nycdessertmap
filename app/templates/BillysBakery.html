Billy's Bakery

Billy's has delicious cupcakes but seems derivative of Magnolia's.

What to get: Banana Nutella cupcake and strawberry lemondade cupcake.

Restaurant Link: Billy's Bakery Website

Menu Link: Billy's Menu

More details: We have mixed feelings about this place. We didn't like that the cupcakes and the atmosphere was a little too similar to Magnolia's. We did think the cupcakes were downright delicious, so we included Billy's on our list because of that deliciousness. Emily got the strawberry lemonade cupcake and Jeff got the banana nutella. Both were among the best cupcakes we've ever had in NYC! The frosting was perfect, the cake was light and fluffy. There was a pretty upbeat atmosphere here on a Saturday night. It seemed like there was a good mix of native New Yorker's coming to their favorite place and tourists looking for one of NYC's best spots. If you're in Chelsea, it's one of your best options for cupcakes!
