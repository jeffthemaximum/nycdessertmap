Best cookies in NYC.

What to get: Oatmeal Raisin Cookie.

Restaurant Link: Levain Bakery Website

Menu Link: Levain Bakery Menu

More details: Levain Bakery sells the best cookies in NYC, period. Their style of cookie is gigantic, thick, with a crispy outside and a gooey inside. On weekends, there's almost always a line but you'll probably get through it in about 10 minutes. The shop is in the basement of a relatively residential Upper West Side building, but Levain is pumping out fresh, warm cookies from the same basement spot that they sell them. This means you'll get a warm, fresh-baked one whenever you go. Flavors include chocolate chip walnut, double chocolate chunk, and oatmeal raisin. Each flavor has a camp of followers who think it's the best, and we tend to think the oatmeal raisin is the bestest of the best. It's buttery and gooey but still crispy and warm, and even the raisins seem just a little bitter than regular raisins, though it may be the fact that they're nessled in a warm Levain cookie that makes them seem exceptional. Regardless, go here, but not too often, or else you may anger your cariologist.
