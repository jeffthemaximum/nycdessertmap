Cafe Lalo

Cafe Lalo is best known for appearing in the hit movie You've Got Mail.

What to get: Emily recommends Maple Pecan Pie and Jeff Recommends Cappuccino Mouse Cake.

Restaurant Link: Cafe Lalo Website

Menu Link: Cafe Lalo Menu

More details: We like the food here and we LOVE the atmosphere. This place has a gigantic menu. It's like 10 pages long, filled with drinks, brunch, desserts, coffees, and even some sandwiches. We recommend wading through the whole menu and just getting some pie or cake, and maybe a coffee drink to wash it down. When you first walk in, you'll immediately see a big glass case with all the pies and cakes they have that day. Spend a couple minutes before you sit down at your table looking over the selection and see if there's any that look particularly awesome to you. What makes the atmosphere unique is the lighting. The place glows like a movie set, which may be why they picked it to appear in You've Got Mail. We recommend going here in the evening, anytime after sunset, so you can fully appreciate the lighting.
