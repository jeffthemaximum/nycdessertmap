Schmackary's Cookies

Delicious cookies that appeal to NYCers and tourists.

What to get: Red Velvet Cookie.

Restaurant Link: Schmackary's Website

Menu Link: Schmackary's Menu

More details: Schmackary's looks like a tourist-trap, times-square type of location, but don't let that fool you. They still make some of the best and funnest cookies we've had anywhere in the city. There's a traditional chocolate chip, but there's also a rotating menu of unique ones, like funfetti, maple bacon, and blueberry cobbler. Emily and I came here at the end of our first epic NYC walking tour, and we'd walked all the way from 168th st. to 45th st. by the time we got to Schmackary's. This cookie spot had an excited mix of people here: there was definitely many tourists, but there was also some suit-and-tie New Yorkers getting a cookie to destress after a workday. The red velvet cookie was thing and big. It was crispy around the perimeter, but it was soft and chewy towards the middle. Our red velvet had a tasty cream cheese frosting, anbd we're always happy when a place combines cookies and frosting. We also thought Schmackary's would be a good dessert treat after a Broadway show, because there's many popular Broadway playhouses nearby.
